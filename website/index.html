How It Works

Three steps to your perfect playlist

AI Reads Your Emotions

DeepFace analyzes every frame, detecting 7 emotions — happiness, sadness, anger, surprise, fear, disgust, and calm — with high accuracy.

Features

Enterprise-grade AI, built for everyone

🎯

Vector-Based Emotion Matching

Uses a full 7-dimension emotion score vector to compute cosine similarity against every song in the catalog — ranked by how closely their mood profile aligns with yours.

📊

Real-time Emotion Timeline

See exactly how your emotions shifted throughout the video. Identify peaks, dips, and your dominant emotional arc with live Chart.js visualization.

⚡

Async Processing Engine

Video processing runs in a background thread so the UI stays responsive. Real-time status polling keeps you updated as each stage completes.

🎛️

BPM & Energy Alignment

Each song is matched not just by emotion but also by tempo and energy level. Intense anger gets high-BPM tracks. Deep sadness gets slow acoustic songs.
