How It Works

Three steps to your
perfect playlist

No account needed. No mood logging. Just your face and your feelings.

📹
Upload a Video

Record yourself for a few seconds or upload any existing clip. Supported formats: MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WEBM. Up to 200MB.

🧠
AI Reads Your Emotions

DeepFace analyzes every frame, detecting 7 emotions — happiness, sadness, anger, surprise, fear, disgust, and calm — with high accuracy.

🎶
Get Your Playlist

Our recommendation engine matches songs to your emotional fingerprint using energy, valence, and BPM alignment. Your mood, your music.

Features

Enterprise-grade AI,
built for everyone

🎯
Vector-Based Emotion Matching

Unlike simple keyword matching, MoodMatch uses the full 7-dimension emotion score vector to compute cosine similarity against every song in the catalog. Songs are ranked by how closely their mood profile aligns with yours — not just one label.

📊
Real-time Emotion Timeline

See exactly how your emotions shifted throughout the video with a live Chart.js visualization. Identify peaks, dips, and your dominant emotional arc.

⚡
Async Processing Engine

Video processing runs in a background thread so the UI stays responsive. Real-time status polling keeps you updated as each stage completes.

🎛️
BPM & Energy Alignment

Each song is matched not just by emotion but also by tempo and energy level. Intense anger gets high-BPM metal. Deep sadness gets slow, low-energy acoustic tracks.

🗃️
Full Session History

Every analysis is saved to a local SQLite database. Review past sessions, compare emotion patterns over time, and revisit old playlists.
